BHAI GURDAS INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY

Bhai Gurdas Institute of Engineering and Technology (BGIET), a premier institution committed to delivering skilled and quality education in line with Global Standards since its inception in 2002. As a proud member of the Bhai Gurdas Group, pioneers in establishing engineering institutions in Punjab, BGIET stands at the forefront of educational excellence. Affiliated with IKG Punjab Technical University, Kapurthala and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi, our group of institution offers programs Engineering & Technology, Management, Medical Sciences, Pharmacy, Education, Agriculture with a steadfast commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society. Our ultimate aim is to cultivate better engineers and contribute to the nation’s advancement by emphasizing the holistic growth of our students, ensuring they are not only academically proficient but also grounded in their human values and principles.
